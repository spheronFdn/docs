## Supported Payment Tokens

These are all the payment tokens that we currently support:

| **Token Name**    | **ICL Config Use** | **Precision** | **Minimum Deposit Amount** |
|:-----------------:|:------------------:|:-------------:|:--------------------------:|
| usd pegged SPON   | uSPON              | 6             | 1 uSPON                    |

The precision is used in the ICL configuration when mentioning the max price for the deployment. 
You need to convert the amount to the precision value before passing it to the ICL deployment.
