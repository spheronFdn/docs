# Deploy Your App on Spheron

This guide will walk you through the steps to deploy and access your app on Spheron. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a successful deployment.

### Step 1: Get Some USDT from the Faucet
1. Visit the [Spheron Faucet](https://faucet.spheron.network/). 
![Spheron Faucet](assets/spheron-faucet.png)
2. Choose the USDT option and Enter your wallet address.
3. Click on the Send Token button.
4. Wait for the transaction to be confirmed. You should now see USDT in your wallet.

### Step 2: Get Some ARB Sepolia ETH

Below, you can find faucets for obtaining testnet ETH. You can use a faucet on Ethereum Sepolia or Arbitrum Sepolia.

| Faucet Operator    | Faucet URL                                    | Chain             |
|--------------------|-----------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| QuickNode          | https://faucet.quicknode.com/arbitrum/sepolia | Arbitrum Sepolia  |
| Alchemy            | https://sepoliafaucet.com/                    | Ethereum Sepolia  |
| Sepolia PoW Faucet | https://sepolia-faucet.pk910.de/              | Ethereum Sepolia  |

### Step 3: Deposit it to Your Escrow Balance
### Step 4: Run Deployment

1. Visit Spheron Network [login page](https://app.spheron.network/login).
2. Choose your preferred authentication method: **Web2 (Google account, GitHub account, GitLab account, or Bitbucket account) or Web3 (Ethereum)**. 
3. Follow the provided prompts to authenticate your chosen account securely. This step ensures safe access to the Spheron Network platform. 
4. Upon logging in, you'll be directed to the next windows where you have to choose **Cloud** option to seamlessly deploy and manage your cloud infrastrucutre.
5. Next, you'll be taken to a new page. Click the **"Create New Projects"** button. Add **'Project Title'** And **'Project Description'** and Click Create.
6. Next, Configure your build settings and environment variables as required.
7. Click on the Deploy button to start the deployment process.

Wait for the deployment to complete. You can monitor the progress in the Deployments tab.

### Step 5: Access Your Deployment

Congratulations! Your app is now deployed and accessible. If you encounter any issues, reach out to Spheron Support.
